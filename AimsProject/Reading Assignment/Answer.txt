Question:

Iterate through the list and print out the information of the media by using the toString() method.

Answer :
Subclasses of Media could be CD, DVD, or Book - And they also have their own implementation of the method ToString
Inheritance lets us inherit attributes and methods from another class. Polymorphism uses those methods to perform different tasks. This allows us to perform a single action in different ways
